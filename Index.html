Gaya

Tutor at Isleworth Music Studio

My name is Gaya and I am a London-based pianist, accompanist, piano and voice teacher,
singer, songwriter and composer. In 2015, after 5 years of teaching as a travelling tutor,
I decided to start Isleworth Music Studio, which since then has welcomed many students
who work towards graded exams or simply learn music for their own enjoyment!

Isleworth Music Studio offers

  • PIANO LESSSONS for all ages and abilities. Classical, Jazz, Pop&Rock music
  • CONTEMPORARY SINGING LESSONS (Rock, Pop, Jazz, Soul, Musical Theatre)
  • MUSIC THEORY (including preparation for graded exams)
  • Accompaniment for solo
    • Accompaniment for graded exams (grades 1-5) - minimum two rehearsals + exam
    • Accompaniment for graded exams (grades 6-8) - minimum two rehearsals
